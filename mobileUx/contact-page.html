About Us

Welcome to the DUMAGETIT App—Your Gateway to Dumaguete’s Finest Finds. We are more than just an app; we are a bridge connecting locals, tourists, and businesses in Dumaguete City to the city’s rich and diverse offerings.

At DUMAGETIT, we take pride in being the digital middleman with a purpose. Our mission is to promote and empower local businesses, artisans, and entrepreneurs by showcasing their products and services to a wider audience. Whether it’s handcrafted goods, local delicacies, unique experiences, or essential services, we curate the best of Dumaguete, making them accessible at your fingertips.

What Sets Us Apart?

  1. Empowering Local Entrepreneurs

    – We’re dedicated to providing a platform for small businesses and local talents to thrive. Every feature and initiative we launch is designed to help Dumaguete’s locals gain the recognition and support they deserve.

  2. Unveiling Hidden Gems

    –We actively collaborate with local businesses and artisans that have yet to gain the spotlight. These under-the-radar talents and enterprises hold immense potential and unique offerings, and we are committed to bringing their stories and products to a wider audience.

  3. Convenience Redefined

    – Whether you’re a resident looking for a trusted service or a tourist eager to explore Dumaguete’s treasures, our app is your reliable companion.

  4. Community-Centric Vision

    – We aim to foster a sense of community and pride by celebrating the cultural heritage and unique identity of Dumaguete City.

    5. Our Commitment:

    DUMAGETIT is built on the values of trust, collaboration, and innovation. By bridging the gap between buyers and sellers, we aim to stimulate local economic growth while giving everyone the opportunity to experience the warmth and creativity of Dumaguete’s people.

    Join us in supporting local businesses and discovering Dumaguete City in its most authentic light. With DUMAGETIT, you’re not just making a transaction—you’re contributing to a thriving community.

    Together, let’s redefine the way we shop, discover, and celebrate Dumaguete’s finest finds!
