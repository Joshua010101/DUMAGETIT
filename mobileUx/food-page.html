SANS RIVAL CAKES AND PASTRIES

Nestled in the heart of Dumaguete City, Sans Rival Cakes & Pastries is a must-visit destination for every traveler, renowned for its world-famous "silvanas". Located along the picturesque Rizal Boulevard, this charming pastry shop has become a culinary landmark, offering visitors a taste of Dumaguete’s sweet heritage. Silvanas, their signature treat, are delicate frozen cookies made of meringue and buttercream, coated with cookie crumbs—a perfect balance of crunch and creaminess. These delectable goodies have gained popularity not just for their unforgettable flavor but also for their role as the quintessential "pasalubong" (gift) for loved ones. Whether you’re savoring them with coffee at the shop or bringing boxes home, silvanas from Sans Rival embody the warmth and sweetness of Dumaguete, making it a truly iconic experience.

Essential Details: Location: Building 5, Dumaguete Public Market, Perdices Street, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental Operating Hours: Daily from 3:30 AM to 5:00 PM

DUMAGUETE'S PAINITAN

Painitan offers a warm and authentic Filipino breakfast experience. Famous for its budbud (sticky rice wrapped in banana leaves) paired with rich, locally made sikwate (hot chocolate), it’s the perfect way to start your day. Whether you're a local or a traveler, this charming spot lets you dive into the heart of Dumaguete’s culture and flavors.

Essential Details: Location: Building 5, Dumaguete Public Market, Perdices Street, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental Operating Hours: Daily from 3:30 AM to 5:00 PM

FOODNET

Tucked away in the heart of the bustling Dumaguete Public Market, the Painitan is more than just a breakfast stop—it’s a cultural and culinary experience that embodies the warmth and simplicity of life in Negros Oriental. Renowned for its signature pairing of budbud, a sticky rice delicacy carefully wrapped in banana leaves, and sikwate, a rich and aromatic hot chocolate made from locally sourced cacao tablea, Painitan is the perfect place to start your day.

Whether you’re a local seeking a comforting meal or a traveler eager to dive into the province’s traditions, the Painitan offers a glimpse into the everyday lives of Dumagueteños. The unpretentious ambiance, with its rustic benches and the lively chatter of morning diners, adds to its charm. For many, it’s not just about the food—it’s about connecting with the community and experiencing the authentic flavors that have been passed down through generations.

Conveniently located in the city center, Painitan is a go-to spot for those wanting to enjoy affordable, satisfying meals while soaking up the vibrant market atmosphere. It’s an iconic part of Dumaguete’s identity and a place where the simplicity of traditional Filipino breakfast is celebrated every single day.

Essential Details: Location: Perdices St, Dumaguete, Negros Oriental Operating Hours: Daily from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM
