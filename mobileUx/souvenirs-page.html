Lee Plaza

With over 27,000 square meters of retail space spread across five floors, we offer everything you need in one convenient location. Whether you're shopping for groceries at our fully stocked supermarket, finding the latest fashion at our department store, or indulging in a treat at our bakery or coffee shop, we've got it all!
