Dive Into Paradise:Apo Island

Explore the pristine waters of Apo Island, renowned for its marine sanctuary. Swim with sea turtles and marvel at vibrant coral reefs in this ecological treasure.

Find Serenity: Balinsasayao Twin Lakes

Escape to the Balinsasayao Twin Lakes, a tranquil oasis for kayaking, hiking, or simply embracing nature’s peace.

A Splash of Adventure: Casaroro Falls

Unleash your adventurous side at Casaroro Falls, a breathtaking cascade hidden within a lush forest—perfect for trekkers.

Uncover History: Silliman University

Step onto the historic grounds of Silliman University, Asia’s first American university, rich in heritage and culture.

Hidden Wonders: Pulangbato Falls

Be captivated by the unique red rock landscapes of Pulangbato Falls, an off-the-beaten-path gem.

Unwind in Elegance: Tierra Alta

Relax at Tierra Alta, a mountain resort offering panoramic views of Dumaguete and luxurious amenities.

Step Back in Time: Rizal Boulevard

Experience nostalgia at Rizal Boulevard, Dumaguete City’s most iconic promenade. With its colonial charm, seaside views, and vibrant street food stalls, it’s a haven for relaxation and local culture.

Relive the Legacy: Quezon Park

Step into the heart of Dumaguete’s story at Quezon Park, where history comes alive. Serene fountain, and lush greenery, it’s a timeless sanctuary that bridges the past and present.

Celebrate Freedom: Ninoy Aquino Freedom Park

Embrace the spirit of democracy at Ninoy Aquino Freedom Park, Dumaguete’s expansive green haven. Perfect for relaxation or recreation, it’s a hub for community events, quiet strolls, and a celebration of unity and resilience.
